Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up on PrestigePages, we collect your first name, last name, email address, password, and mobile number. We also store the date your account was created in our database (Firebase Firestore).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to create and manage your account, send OTPs for verification via EmailJS, and improve our services. We do not sell or share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored using Google Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. EmailJS is used solely for secure email-based OTP delivery.

4. Cookies and Tracking

PrestigePages may use cookies or local storage to maintain your login session and preferences. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us at: privacy@prestigepages.com

6. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (by Google) and EmailJS to provide account authentication and email delivery. These services may collect limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related concerns, reach us at: privacy@prestigepages.com
